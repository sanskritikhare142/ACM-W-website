So, what do we do?

Discourse

Discussing ideas are at the core of any community, we hold regular sessions where we talk about varying things from research papers to project ideas. The ‘Discourse’ is a great way to initiate intellectual dialogue between the members of our community.

Colloquy

Workshops are a way for our community members to perfect their skills by sharing their knowledge and providing a hands-on experience to those interested. We also frequently bring in industry experts to conduct our workshops.

Cicerone

The journey to learn and become better in Computing can often be a scary ordeal. Our mentor and mentee system enables us to provide support to those who are just getting started in the field.
